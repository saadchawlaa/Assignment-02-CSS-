Pakistan Cricket Team

The Pakistan national cricket team, often referred to as the Shaheens ('Falcons'), Green Shirts, Men in Green and Cornered Tigers is administered by the Pakistan Cricket Board (PCB). The team is a Full Member of the International Cricket Council, and participates in Test, One Day International (ODI) and Twenty20 International cricket matches.
